Ranu is bringing authentic Indian cuisine to San Francisco

Ranu's decades long career has spanned a delectable saga across many countries. Ranu loved to cook as a teenager, and opened her first restaurant in his native Nepal before sojourning to work at an Indian restaurant in Korea. Chef Ranu then journeyed over to the States, where she honed his craft, capping his culinary journey off with stints at tech giants Apple and Dropbox. While at Dropbox, Ranu cooked for many celebrities including Justin Timberlake, Kanye West, Condoleeza Rice, the prime minister of Singapore, and Jon Ying. And today, she works to bring together everything she's learned in his worldly travels in order to serve you the best Indian food you've ever had.
